Travel care

DELIVERY & GUARANTEES

The puppy is delivered within 1–3 weeks after the deposit is made (timing depends on the puppy's age and the readiness of all required documents).

Each puppy travels to its new home only after full vaccination, quarantine completion, a veterinary health check, and confirmed fitness for travel.

Every stage is carefully organized with full transparency and attention to detail — from reservation to handover. The entire process is designed so you feel calm, supported, and fully informed at every step.

01

Delivery Planning & Schedule

  • Important notice: The exact arrival date and time are provided by the courier service 2–5 days before departure.
  • All timelines and details are always agreed and confirmed in advance.
  • After reserving a puppy, we book the delivery service and confirm approximate arrival dates with you beforehand, so everything is clear and well planned from the start.
  • Standard delivery is arranged through our trusted logistics partners and follows a carefully planned route for safety and comfort.
  • Individual delivery (VIP option) is available upon request — fully personalized scheduling based on your preferred day and time. This option is arranged separately with your manager.
02

Puppy Travel Preparation

The puppy is released for travel only after:

  • age-appropriate full vaccination
  • completed quarantine period
  • full veterinary examination
  • confirmed health clearance and travel readiness
03

Documents

Each puppy arrives with:

  • European veterinary passport
  • microchip registration

If required, FCI or IKU documentation can be arranged.

Export pedigree documentation is also agreed upon in advance with your manager.

04

Transport & Hand Delivery

We work exclusively with a trusted and highly reliable courier service specializing in safe and gentle pet transportation.

The puppy travels personally from my hands into yours.

This is a carefully controlled and fully managed handover process, where every step is supervised with the highest level of care and responsibility.

  • Flight Nanny (in-cabin escort) — the puppy travels in the cabin under professional supervision and is handed directly to the owner
  • Cargo delivery (animal transport compartment) — available for certain countries in accordance with airline regulations

The route and delivery method are selected individually, based on safety, comfort, and destination requirements.

Additional care

From reservation to full adaptation in the new home, we remain in contact and provide guidance on feeding, care, and adjustment, ensuring a smooth and gentle transition for your puppy.
