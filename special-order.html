Reservation & Cancellation Policy

After selecting and reserving a specific puppy, cancellation or refusal is not possible after 2 days from the reservation date.

If you decide to cancel after this period, the deposit is non-refundable, as expenses related to puppy care, transportation arrangements, and courier booking services will already have been incurred.

If a proposed puppy does not meet your expectations, you may wait for another option that matches your search criteria. Your deposit will remain active as part of the puppy search service.
